My Favorite Coffee Tools

Here’s a look at the coffee tools I use regularly to make great drinks at home. You don't need all of them, but each one serves a wonderful purpose in the pursuit of the perfect cup.

On This Page

  • Moka Pot
  • AeroPress

Moka Pot

A classic stovetop coffee maker that produces a strong, espresso-like brew. It's an affordable and durable way to make rich coffee without a large machine.

AeroPress

A versatile and portable coffee maker beloved for its ability to brew smooth, full-flavored coffee with low acidity. It's fast, easy to clean, and great for experimenting.
